Picoclash

A grid-based turn-based 2D strategy game.
download .ZIPdownload .TGZ

Picoclash is a strategy game that attempts to bring the battle mechanics from 2D strategy roleplaying games (SRPGs) to your personal computer.

Running Picoclash

These instructions assume you are running Linux. First you will need to download SDL and its extensions, SDL_image, SDL_ttf, and SDL_mixer. Once you have those installed through your favorite package manager, download the Picoclash source using either of the links above and extract. Then in your terminal:

$ cd picoclash
$ make
$ ./Picoclash

Creating your own maps

Each character represents a square in the map. The key is found below:

w: blue warrior
a: blue archer
h: blue healer
W: red warrior
A: red archer
H: red healer
o: rock
.: grass
